Stefania Lazar was educated at University of Łódź where she received her Master degree in Theatre studies and Art Promotion. She is a stylist and costume designer. She works as an art director on the photoshoots for the top fashion magazines in Poland. She also manages and creates fashion show concepts and planning, which she also performs for other cultural events.

Acted as a 2nd costume designer on a movie directed by Jan Jakub Kolski, „Ułaskawienie” - which in 2018 received an award for best costumes during 43rd edition of The Gdynia Film Festival (Festiwal Polskich Filmów Fabularnych w Gdyni).

Worked as a lead costume designer on her first feature film, „Żużel” - directed by Dorota Kędzierzawska. She worked for companies such as: Lotto, Allegro Smart, Discovery Channel, Seni Polska, Tax Solidarity Eu.

As a stylist and art director she works on photoshoots for the most popular magazines, including: K Mag, Harper's Bazaar, L’Officiel, Wysokie Obcasy, or Nasty Magazine. She was a co-creator of Fashion Philosophy Fashion Week Poland and KTW Fashion Week (ELLE Style Award 2019).

Directed fashion shows for brands such as: BLIKVANGER, NATASHA PAVLUCHENKO, MMC STUDIO, ODIO TEES, BIZUU, TOMASZ OSSOLIŃSKI, SOWIK, JAROSŁAW EWERT.

Believes in the power of detail and the idea of communication throughout a costume.

Likes to create the world around her by mixing gestures, motion, textures and colors.

During photoshoots that she organizes, her main goal is to make sure that her models present the emotions that the show requires. She does so via particular motion and styling - two most important markers that, when combined, allow Stefania to present her unique vision.
